Comprehensive Epoxy Flooring Calgary Services

At Elegant Coatings, we provide a diverse range of concrete floor coatings tailored to meet various needs. Our expert services include:

Polyaspartic Coatings

Polyaspartic coatings are known for their rapid curing times and exceptional resistance to UV rays, chemicals, and abrasion. Ideal for high-traffic areas, they deliver a tough, long-lasting finish that performs well even in the most demanding conditions.

Epoxy Floor Coatings

Perfect for both residential and commercial spaces, our epoxy floor coating offer a seamless, high-gloss finish that combines durability and aesthetics. This versatile option is ideal for garages, basements, warehouses, and more.

Polyurethane Coatings

For environments requiring resilient and long-lasting flooring, polyurethane coatings are an excellent choice. Their superior resistance to abrasion and impact makes them ideal for spaces with heavy use, and their flexibility ensures performance in areas with fluctuating temperatures.

Metallic Epoxy

Create a bold and sophisticated look with our metallic epoxy coatings. These finishes provide a reflective, dynamic appearance, adding depth and a luxurious touch to any space.

Flake Systems

Our flake systems enhance both the appearance and functionality of your floors. These coatings feature textured flakes that add color and slip resistance, making them a great choice for garages, patios, and other areas where safety and style are essential.

Quartz Systems

Combining the durability of epoxy with the added strength of quartz granules, quartz systems are ideal for industrial and commercial spaces requiring heavy-duty flooring solutions. They offer unparalleled performance and durability.
